DESIGN | TECHNOLOGY | RESEARCH

I am a non-defined designer and like to try new things. Besides, I am always enthusiastic about integrating design and technologies together to solve problems. This space collects my ideas and works. Here you may find not only my serious thinkings, but also some useless funny projects. If you have any thoughts, don't hesitate to contact me!
