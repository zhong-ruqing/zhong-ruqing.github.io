[Arch Design] Pause Space

Design Project | Architecture | In Collaboration with Dongyuan Liu | 2018


Building rural toilet with local material.
The scope of Toilet Revolution in China has been extended beyond tourist attractions to the whole country, urban areas to countryside since 2015. In response to the appeal of Toilet Revolution, this project aims to explore the space possibilities of a rural toilet with local green material, bamboo.
