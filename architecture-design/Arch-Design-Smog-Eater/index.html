[Arch Design] Smog Eater

Master Thesis | University of Stuttgart | Architecture and Urban Planning | 2019


An adaptive modular skin system against air pollution.
Concept of this project is to design a modular architectural skin system, which can absorb polluted air and deliver purified air out. This skin system can be distributed on different kinds of “surfaces” in city. By adapting itself to different shapes and environment conditions, it can not only be added on existing buildings, be integrated in new buildings and even be designed as installations in public urban spaces to help purifying the polluted air in the whole city.
