[Arch Design] City Art Center

Undergraduate Design Project | Zhejiang University | Architecture | In Collaboration with Mengying Hu | 2016


An art center breaks the boundary between city and nature.
This project locates at the boundary of the nature and city in Hangzhou. The original buildings that exist here have cut the contact of people and nature because of the height and arrangement. The concept of this project is to break the boundary between city and nature as well as the barrier between the citizens and art here.
